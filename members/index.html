Welcome KING...

King's Keep is your setup guide and resource hub. Follow the five steps below to get fully connected, ready to mint POAPs, and ready to battle with the clan.

5-Step Setup

1

Connect with Leaders in Supercell Store

Add the clan leaders in Supercell Store so you can stay connected for updates, rewards, and invites.

2

Create a Wallet for POAPs

Set up a wallet so your POAPs are safe and easy to manage long-term. Ask a leader if you need help choosing one.

3

Install POAP Home

Install POAP Home. This is where you will mint and manage POAPs you receive while in the clan.

4

Share Info for the Clan Website

Submit the Google Form shared by leaders so your member details can be added to the clan website roster.

5

Win Battles

Push ladder, show up in wars when you can, support clanmates, and collect your seasonal proof.
