FAQ

Common questions about POAP KINGS, clan life, and how POAPs work.

Clan Life

Do I have to do Clan Wars?

No. Wars are encouraged, but we value consistent participation and good vibes over strict requirements.

How do I earn Elder?

Donate consistently and help clanmates. Elder is earned and can be removed if donations stop long-term.

How is War Champ chosen?

Primarily by Clan Wars contribution for the season. Leaders break ties with common sense and transparency.

Is this affiliated with Supercell?

No. POAP KINGS is a fan community and not affiliated with Supercell.

Nope. We'll keep claiming simple. If you're new, we'll help you get set up.

How do I collect my POAP?

Active members receive a claim link at the end of each season. Follow the link and mint your POAP to your wallet. If you don't have a wallet yet, check out our Clan Setup guide.

What is the Founder POAP?

A special one-time POAP for members who join within the first two months of the clan. It marks you as part of POAP KINGS from the very beginning.
